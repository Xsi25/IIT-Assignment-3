Our ideal jobs

There are a range of ideal jobs in our group, including full stack developer, software engineer, UX/UI designer and Channels Director. Despite the diversity of job titles, there are significant similarities in both the general and technical skills and knowledge required for most of these roles.

Technically, four out of five of the roles required knowledge of programming languages, and three required knowledge of modern web development frameworks, cross platform abilities and experience with Agile methods. In terms of general skills, all of the roles require well-developed communication skills (written/oral), team work skills and interpersonal skills. There was also a strong call for project management skills, research and analysis skills, and passion and enthusiasm in the respective advertisements. Four out of five of the roles explicitly required the completion of at least a Bachelor degree.

The main differences between roles can be seen in the specific technical skills required in some areas, for example, UX/UI design skills, wireframing and prototyping, and cloud experience, and in the relative importance of leadership skills. One of the roles is a senior management role, which is different from the other four roles that are more narrowly focused on specific and technical IT roles. The management role requires more than 10 years of experience, where the other roles require 0-5 years of experience. In addition, only the senior management position requires the completion of postgraduate business qualifications.

While the job titles vary, the analysis of the team’s ideal job advertisements reveals significant similarities in terms of both the general and specific skills that we need to develop to succeed in obtaining these roles.
