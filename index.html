A little bit about David

I’m an Australian currently completing a Bachelor of IT with a minor in Digital Design at RMIT. My interest in IT was sparked early, playing around on my father’s Commodore 64 with floppy drive. I love to design and build things, and enjoy researching, designing and producing innovative PC builds. I’m fascinated by many things in IT, including network security programming and the dynamic PC hardware landscape.

I have experience as an electrical systems technician, troubleshooting and reparing hardware and software for parking meters, and am the go-to person for Windows trouble shooting and technical support for family and friends.

Hobbies

I enjoy researching, designing and producing innovative PC builds, creating digital art, and online gaming. I am a board game fanatic, my collection spans 1200 titles and my favorite genres are legacy/campaign, war, area control and heavy Euro games.

Personality

Myers Briggs: INFJ VARK: equal preference for Auditory and Kinesthetic Big 5 personality: Empathetic idealist – analytical thinker The test results highlight my preference for structure and organisation which I can use to help the team organise work flow and keep on track for deadlines. Having an awareness of my test results might provide other members of the group insight into how and why I approach the project and tasks in a particular way.

A little bit about Hao
Born in China, grew up in Australia, and got to enjoy the best of both worlds. Fluent in English and can speak a decent amount of Mandarin Chinese. My interest in IT began since I first moved to Australia when I was 6 years old. It began when I went to my first computer class in grade 2, I saw a whole bunch of Apple IIe computers lined up on the desks. When I turned it on and started tapping away at the old keyboards, seeing the screen light up, I was hooked. From then on, I started learning as much as I could from class, books, magazines and through trial and error whenever given the chance.

My IT experience mostly involve Windows and Office 365 troubleshooting support. I have some experience in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator and Dreamweaver from personal interest, but they’re not needed in my current job.

Hobbies
I’m a huge fan of all types of computer games, wasting countless hours away in Dota 2, World of Warcraft and multiple coop titles. I also enjoy cooking and trying out new recipes that I find off websites like Yummly. It’s a great feeling seeing friends and family try and enjoy new cuisines through food that I’ve made.

Personality
Myer-Briggs Test Result Myers Briggs: Assertive Architect (INTJ-A) Online Learning Styles: The Visual Learner Creativity Test: Creativity Score of 68.38 My test results reflect my inclination towards working independently and solving problems strategically. I’m happy to be given a task and be allowed the freedom to complete it without outside interference. While I do my best to work with colleagues in group situations, I prefer meetings to be direct and to the point so that I can get back to work on my tasks. That said, communication will be key in this project and it’s something I will be mindful of while working with my team members.

I have grown up in Australia specifically Cairns, I now live in Victoria studying at RMIT and working as a meat inspector.
